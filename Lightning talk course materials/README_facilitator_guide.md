## Tips for a Powerful Demo

- **Don't hide the prompts.** Show them on screen — the audience needs to see how simple they are.
- **Use the silence.** When Claude is generating, let the 15-second pause land. Don't fill it with talking. The speed IS the demo.
- **React authentically.** If something Claude writes is unexpectedly good, say so. Genuine reactions are contagious.
- **The Q&A setup:** After Lesson 3, ask "Who here has written a call note they were proud of?" Pause. "With Claude Cowork, every call note is your best call note."
