*For use with Lesson 2 Demo: "Emails That Lead with Insight"*

---

## Demo Prompt to Use in Claude Cowork
> "Using the news hook and prospect context below, write a cold outreach email to Marcus Johnson at Caterpillar. Lead with the insight about their autonomous mining announcement — not with NVIDIA features. The email should be under 120 words, end with a specific question, and feel like it came from someone who did their homework."

---

## The News Hook (paste this in)
**Headline:** *"Caterpillar Announces 'Cat Autonomy' Platform for Mining Sites — Plans to Deploy 500 Autonomous Trucks by 2027"*
Source: Caterpillar Investor Day Presentation, February 18, 2026

Key details from the announcement:
- Cat Autonomy will require real-time inference at the edge (on each truck) AND centralized model training at HQ
- CEO Jim Umpleby: *"Our biggest bottleneck is not the trucks — it's the AI. We need to train models 3x faster than today."*
- Caterpillar plans to train on 2.5 billion sensor data points per quarter from global mining operations
- Timeline: First 100 autonomous trucks in production by Q4 2026 — AI training milestone must be hit by Q3 2026

---

## Prospect Profile: Marcus Johnson
- **Title:** Senior Director, Digital Transformation, Caterpillar Inc.
- **Location:** Peoria, Illinois (HQ)
- **Background:** 14 years at Caterpillar; mechanical engineer turned digital transformation leader; MBA Kellogg 2018
- **Known priorities (from LinkedIn + press):** Reducing time-to-deploy on industrial AI, proving ROI of digital transformation to the board
- **Communication preference:** Practical, ROI-focused; allergic to buzzword-heavy vendor pitches
- **Last interaction with NVIDIA:** Attended NVIDIA GTC 2025, visited the DGX Cloud booth, no follow-up after

---

## Why NVIDIA Fits the Caterpillar Moment
- The "3x faster training" CEO goal maps directly to H200 cluster vs. their current Azure GPU setup
- Edge inference for autonomous trucks = NVIDIA Jetson Orin (already in 200+ industrial edge deployments)
- Cat Autonomy timeline (Q3 2026 AI milestone) creates urgency — 5 months away
- NVIDIA has 3 reference customers in industrial autonomy: John Deere (autonomous tractors), Komatsu (mining AI), Volvo CE (construction site autonomy)

---

## What to Avoid in the Email
- Don't open with "I saw you're doing AI" — too generic
- Don't mention GPU specs in the first email
- Don't pitch DGX Cloud before understanding whether they prefer CapEx or OpEx
- Don't use "synergies," "leverage," or "cutting-edge"
