## Background and Business Need

Meridian's sales organization is undergoing significant growth. We plan to hire 18 new enterprise AEs in Q2 2026 as part of a strategic expansion into new verticals and geographies. Based on historical data, our current average AE ramp time — defined as time from hire date to 75% quota attainment — is 8.5 months.

This ramp time creates a material business problem. Internal analysis completed in Q1 2026 estimated that deals lost or delayed during the first 6 months of an AE's tenure represented approximately $2.1M in missed revenue in FY25. With 18 new AEs coming on board, we project a similar or larger exposure in FY26 unless the ramp experience is meaningfully improved.

We are evaluating vendors who can demonstrably reduce AE ramp time while maintaining deal quality and customer experience standards.

---

## Scope of Work

We are seeking a sales readiness platform that addresses the following needs:

### 1. Onboarding Acceleration
- Structured onboarding curriculum with assessments and certification milestones
- Product and solution knowledge modules (must support complex industrial/manufacturing context)
- Territory and account familiarization tools

### 2. Ongoing Coaching and Skill Development
- Call recording integration and coaching workflows
- Manager-to-rep coaching tools that scale without requiring proportional manager time
- Peer learning from top performer call libraries

### 3. CRM and Workflow Integration
- Deep integration with Salesforce (our CRM — currently mid-migration to Salesforce Enterprise)
- Contextual nudges and alerts within the Salesforce workflow — reps should not need to switch tools
- Ability to surface relevant content and coaching at key deal stages

### 4. Analytics and Measurement
- Ramp time tracking by cohort with dashboards visible to VP, regional directors, and rep level
- Leading indicators of ramp success (early-stage pipeline activity, call quality scores)
- ROI methodology with baseline measurement capability

## Specific Questions for Vendors

Please address each question directly in your proposal:

1. **Ramp time reduction**: What is the average ramp time improvement your customers achieve? Provide 2–3 reference customers in manufacturing, industrials, or complex B2B sales with verifiable results. Include baseline and post-implementation figures.

2. **Salesforce migration**: We are mid-Salesforce migration (expected completion Q3 2026). Describe how your platform handles a mid-migration environment. What is the integration approach during the transition period?

3. **Manager capacity**: Our regional directors manage 8–12 AEs each. They cannot significantly increase time-per-rep. How does your platform scale coaching without adding to manager workload?

4. **Implementation timeline**: What is your standard implementation timeline for a 140-person commercial organization? What are the key milestones, and what do you require from our team?

5. **Measurement methodology**: How do you establish a baseline and measure ramp time improvement? How do you attribute improvement to your platform vs. other variables?
