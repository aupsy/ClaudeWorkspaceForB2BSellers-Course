## Module 2: Account Intelligence — Discovery Is a Research Problem

**The coaching truth**: Discovery quality is a research problem as much as a questioning technique problem. You cannot ask insightful questions about a business you don't understand.

**O → E → A framework**:
- **Orientation** — Open with a credibility statement. Show you understand their situation before asking them to explain it.
- **Exploration** — Current state → Business impact → What would change if the problem went away?
- **Advancement** — Summarize in their exact words. Set next step live, before you hang up.

## Module 3: Outreach That Gets Responses

**The coaching truth**: Trust is built through demonstrated understanding — not charm, not persistence. *Insight personalization* (showing you understand their specific business situation) is what creates trust. *Surface personalization* (mentioning their college, mutual connections) signals shallow research.

## Module 5: The AI Seller Mindset — Amplifier, Not Crutch

**The coaching truth**: The reps who get the most out of AI are the ones who use it to think harder, not to think less. The tasks AI handles well (research, drafting, data entry) are table stakes. The tasks that create competitive advantage are becoming *more human*: complex judgment, genuine curiosity, relationship depth.
