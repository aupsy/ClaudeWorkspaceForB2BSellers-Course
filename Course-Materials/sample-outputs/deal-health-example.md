### Top 3 Risk Factors

**Risk 1: The economic buyer is not activated (Critical)**
Diana Reyes has seen a 10-minute demo. She has no business case, no ROI analysis, and no reason to prioritize this. If procurement goes to her cold, she'll either stall it or send it back to legal indefinitely. Marcus needs to brief her before the next formal touchpoint — ideally with a 1-page ROI summary we prepare for him.

**Risk 2: Legal/IT review has gone dark for 26 days**
Legal started a review on Feb 10. No update since Feb 18. This is either: (a) legal is fine and no one told us, (b) there's a new objection Marcus isn't surfacing, or (c) IT raised the data residency concern and it's blocking. The silence itself is a signal. We need to know which scenario this is.

**Risk 3: We never sent the ROI deck**
The business case was built verbally in the demo. Marcus accepted the math. But "accepted the math in a demo" is not a selling document that travels to the CFO or legal. Without a written ROI summary, Diana Reyes has nothing to reference, and Marcus has nothing to send. This is a gap we created.
