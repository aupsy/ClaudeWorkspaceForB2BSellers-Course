# Sample Cold Emails — Personalization Audit Set

*10 fictional cold emails at varying quality levels, for use in the Module 3 Personalization Audit exercise.*
*Companies and names are fictional. All are selling a sales readiness/enablement platform.*

---

**Instructions**: Paste all 10 emails into Claude chat with this prompt:

```
Analyze these 10 cold emails. For each one, score it 1–5 on:
- Specificity of the trigger referenced (1 = generic observation, 5 = highly specific event)
- Quality of the problem hypothesis (1 = feature push, 5 = genuine business insight)
- CTA quality (1 = vague "let me know", 5 = specific, low-friction ask)
Then tell me: what patterns do you see across all 10? What would the best rep at a top
sales org do differently? Give me 3 specific rules to apply to my next batch of outreach.
```
