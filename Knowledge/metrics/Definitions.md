# Metric Definitions

Standard definitions for the metrics we track. Use these consistently in pipeline reviews, forecasts, and QBRs.

---

## Revenue Metrics

**ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)**
New or expanded contract value, annualized. A $150K 2-year deal = $75K ARR.

**ACV (Annual Contract Value)**
Same as ARR for annual contracts. For multi-year deals, ACV = total value ÷ years.

**NRR (Net Revenue Retention)**
(Starting ARR + Expansion ARR - Churn ARR - Contraction ARR) ÷ Starting ARR × 100
> 100% = growing existing base. Target: [FILL IN — e.g., 115%]

**GRR (Gross Revenue Retention)**
Starting ARR excluding expansion ÷ Starting ARR × 100. Measures pure churn.

---

## Pipeline Metrics

**Pipeline Coverage**
Total pipeline value ÷ Remaining quota. Standard target: 3x.
Example: $300K remaining quota, $900K in pipeline = 3x coverage.

**Weighted Pipeline**
Each deal's value × probability to close (by stage). More realistic than raw pipeline.

**Pipeline Created**
New opportunities opened in a given period. Leading indicator of future revenue.

**Sales Velocity**
(Number of deals × Average deal size × Win rate) ÷ Average sales cycle length
Measures how much revenue you generate per day.

---

## Win/Loss Metrics

**Win Rate**
Deals won ÷ (Deals won + Deals lost) × 100
Exclude deals where no decision was made or prospect went dark.

**Win Rate vs. Competitors**
Deals won in competitive evaluations ÷ All competitive evaluations × 100
Track separately — reveals competitive health vs. overall process health.

**Average Sales Cycle**
Average days from opportunity created to closed-won.
Track separately for deal sizes if range is wide (SMB vs. Enterprise may differ 2x).

---

## Activity Metrics

**Discovery calls booked**: New first-time discovery calls in a given week/month
**Demos delivered**: Full product demonstrations (after discovery)
**Proposals sent**: Written proposals or pricing delivered
**Forecast calls**: Regular cadence of pipeline review with manager

**Sequencing ratios** (benchmarks):
- 100 outbound touches → 10-15 discovery calls (10-15% connect rate)
- 10 discovery calls → 4-5 qualified opportunities (40-50% qualification rate)
- 5 qualified opportunities → 1-2 closed deals (20-40% win rate, varies by segment)

## Deal Stage Definitions

*Customize these to match your CRM stages.*

| Stage | Definition | Probability |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| [Prospecting] | Identified as target; outreach not yet started | 5% |
| [Discovery] | Discovery call completed; pain identified | 15% |
| [Qualified] | MEDDIC mostly complete; clear path to close | 25% |
| [Technical Evaluation] | Demo + proof of concept; technical validation | 40% |
| [Proposal] | Proposal or pricing submitted | 60% |
| [Negotiation] | Commercial and/or legal negotiation underway | 80% |
| [Verbal Commit] | Verbal yes; contract being prepared | 90% |
| [Closed-Won] | Signed contract received | 100% |
| [Closed-Lost] | Explicitly lost or prospect chose no decision | 0% |

---

## Forecast Categories

**Commit**: Deals you are highly confident will close this period. You would bet your quota on these.
**Upside**: Deals that could close if things go well. Real opportunities, some uncertainty.
**Best Case**: Everything in your pipeline that has a shot — optimistic scenario.
**Pipeline**: All open opportunities regardless of close probability.
