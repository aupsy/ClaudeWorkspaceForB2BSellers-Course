# Product Overview — Apex Sales Intelligence

## What We Sell

**Apex Sales Intelligence** is a sales readiness platform that helps B2B sales organizations reduce AE and SDR ramp time, improve discovery call quality, and build repeatable revenue motions at scale.

We combine AI-powered call analysis, contextual coaching delivered in the rep's workflow, and a structured knowledge base so that best practices from top performers automatically flow to the whole team — without requiring proportional manager time.

---

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Company size**: 200–2,000 employees
**Industry**: Mid-market and enterprise B2B, particularly SaaS, professional services, manufacturing (complex B2B sale)
**Revenue**: $50M–$1B ARR
**Primary buyer**: VP of Sales or CRO (economic buyer), Sales Operations or Enablement Manager (champion)
**Trigger signals**:
- Hiring surge (>5 net new AEs or SDRs in a quarter)
- New CRO or VP of Sales (first 90 days)
- CRM migration or sales methodology change
- Miss on quota for 2+ consecutive quarters attributable to rep performance
- Growing performance disparity between top and bottom quartile reps

---

## Key Differentiators

### 1. In-workflow coaching (not a separate LMS)
Coaching happens inside Salesforce, not in a tool reps visit once a quarter. Contextual nudges appear at key deal stages — before a demo, when a deal stalls, when a new stakeholder is added. Reps don't context-switch; managers don't chase.

### 2. Performance pattern analysis from actual calls
Apex analyzes call recordings to surface what top performers do differently — specific questions they ask, how they handle objections, when they introduce pricing. This gets packaged into coaching content automatically, without the enablement team having to build it manually.

### 3. Ramp time measurement built in
We baseline ramp time at onboarding and track it throughout the ramp period. Managers see leading indicators (call quality scores, pipeline activity, early deal progression) — not just lagging indicators (quota attainment at 6 months). This allows coaching interventions before it's too late.

### 4. Salesforce-native (not an integration)
We're built on top of Salesforce's platform — not bolted on. Implementation doesn't require a data migration or custom API work. For organizations mid-CRM migration, we can deploy incrementally.

## Competitive Positioning

### vs. Highspot / Seismic (content management)
Highspot and Seismic excel at content management and distribution. We're a coaching and ramp platform, not a content library. We're not competitive — many customers use both. If a rep has the content but doesn't know how to use it in a conversation, that's where Apex adds value.

### vs. Gong / Chorus (conversation intelligence)
Gong and Chorus capture and transcribe calls. We use call data for coaching and skill development, not just analysis and reporting. Many customers feed Gong data into Apex for coaching workflows. Complementary, not competitive.

### vs. Mindtickle / Brainshark (traditional LMS)
Traditional LMS tools are used for onboarding compliance but rarely drive sustained behavior change. The content is separate from the workflow and the feedback loop is slow. Apex is in-workflow, tied to live deal data, and self-reinforcing over time.
