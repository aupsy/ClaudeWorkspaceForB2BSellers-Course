## How to Use Competitive Intel

1. **During discovery**: Surface competitor usage early. "What tools are you currently using for [X]? Are you evaluating any other solutions?"
2. **During evaluation**: Don't lead with attacks. Win on strengths, then address gaps.
3. **In proposals**: Reference their stated criteria — show how we meet each one better.
4. **In late stage**: If you lose ground to a competitor, involve your manager and pull the battle card immediately.

---

## Competitive Positioning Principles

- **Lead with your strengths**, not their weaknesses
- **Make them prove it** — "That's interesting, how does [Competitor] handle [edge case]?"
- **Use customers as proof** — reference customers who evaluated and chose us over the competitor
- **Never badmouth competitors** — it makes you look insecure; let the product speak
