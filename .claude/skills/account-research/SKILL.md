# Account Research Skill

Deep research on any company to prep for outreach or a first meeting. Usage: `/account-research [Company Name]`

## Instructions

When invoked with a company name:

### Step 1: Check for Existing Context

Look for an existing account folder in `Accounts/`. If found, read `Account-Overview.md` to understand what we already know and avoid duplicating it.

### Step 2: Research the Company

Use web search to gather:

1. **Company basics**: What they do, size, revenue, recent growth/funding, leadership
2. **Recent news** (last 6 months): Acquisitions, layoffs, expansions, earnings announcements, exec changes, product launches
3. **Business challenges**: Industry headwinds, competitive pressures, stated strategic priorities (check investor presentations, earnings calls, press releases)
4. **Buying signals**: Hiring patterns (are they scaling sales?), technology investments, recent announcements that indicate budget and priority
5. **Key people**: Current VP Sales / CRO / Sales Ops (LinkedIn, company website, press releases)

### Step 3: Connect to Our Product

Read `Knowledge/product/Product-Overview.md` to understand:
- Our ICP and what buying triggers indicate fit
- The problems we solve and which of their challenges map to those
